Beneath the Surface

Beneath the waves, another world begins.

Scroll to dive in

Everything we leave behind eventually finds its way here. Plastic drifts across the surface, carried by waves and currents for years. Long before we notice the damage, marine life already feels the consequences.

Coral reefs, sea turtles and thousands of species depend on healthy oceans to survive. This underwater world is full of life, movement and beauty — yet it becomes more fragile every day.

The deeper we go, the ocean becomes quieter and colder. Pollution continues sinking deeper, reaching places humans rarely even see.

What sinks beneath the surface doesn’t disappear. The deepest parts of the ocean have become a graveyard for human waste.
